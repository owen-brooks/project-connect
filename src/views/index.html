Welcome to Project Connect!

Project Connect allows students to connect with one another in order to bring their ideas to life. By adding your skills, background, and interests to your profile, you can match with projects that are in need of your talents. Alternatively, you can create your own project that has a set of required skills in order to meet others who can fulfill them.

Getting Started

Get started by creating your own project. By listing out necessary skills and a thorough description, you can connect with others who can help you get the job done. If you are looking to help out with other projects, either connect with projects we recommend for you based on your talents, , or search for a project by its title, skills needed, or both.
